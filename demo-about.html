About Us

Our Mission

WE PROVIDE OUR CLIENTS WITH DESIGN, IMPLEMENTATION AND SUPPORT SERVICES TO PRODUCE HIGH VALUE BUSINESS OUTCOMES.

CIO Federal IT began in 2010, under the leadership of President Anthony Clayton and his years of prior program management work. Working closely with government agencies throughout the Justice and Judicial systems our team can provide the knowledge and skills to seamlessly manage any project. Focusing on our employees and the people we work with, CIO Federal IT is committed to delivering high value outcomes to our clients.

Our President

ANTHONY CLAYTON IS THE OWNER/OPERATOR AND SENIOR CONSULTANT FOR CIO FEDERAL IT, A FULL-SERVICE IT INFRASTRUCTURE AND PROJECT MANAGEMENT COMPANY SERVING THE FEDERAL AND CIVILIAN SECTORS.

After several years with companies involved in the Department of Justice and Department of Homeland Security, Mr. Clayton opened CIO Federal IT knowing his skills and leadership would provide a platform for the company to grow on. His extensive background in program and IT infrastructure management provides guidance to all aspects of the company. His drive and dedication to his work has become a core fundamental of CIO Federal IT, resulting in a business model to sustain market strength.

Mr. Clayton has developed a reputation as a strong communicator, business developer and a proven PMP™. Specific areas of specialties include IT operations and maintenance, systems development lifecycle, operational improvement and contract management. Along with the over 25 years of leadership experience his team brings, Mr. Clayton and CIO Federal IT can provide the directions any IT or program implementation your organization needs.
